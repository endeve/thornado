thornado-hydro: a discontinuous Galerkin method for supernova hydrodynamics with nuclear equations of state

We describe the methods for solving the equations of nonrelativistic hydrodynamic in thornado with a general, nuclear equation of state. We provide descriptions of the discontinuous Galerkin spatial discretization, the strong stability preserving Runge-Kutta time integration, and limiters necessary for a stable solution. A suite of hydrodynamic tests are performed to show the accuracy and stability of thornado. To test the implementation on a problem relevant to core-collapse supernovae, we apply thornado to the adiabatic gravitational collapse of a 15 solar mass progenitor, following the collapse and through bouce.
